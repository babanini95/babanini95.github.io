[**Party Barrage**](https://party-barage-web.vercel.app/) Party Barrage is an blockchain-based action game blending 3 VS 3 MOBA and Real time Strategy. It's developed by [**Miracle Gates Entertainment**](https://www.miraclegates.com/). I was appointed as the programmer lead for this project. Unfortunately, due to some issues, the project was not able to be published.
<!--more-->

## Contributions

As the programmer lead, I was responsible for overseeing the technical development of the game by my 3 team members, coordinating with the design and art teams, and ensuring that our implementation aligned with our the project requirements. Aside for leading the team, I also contributed to:

- Implementing core gameplay mechanics and systems using Unity and Photon Fusion for multiplayer networking.
- Developing smart contracts in Solidity to manage in-game assets and economies on the blockchain.
- Integrating Sequence SDK for wallet management and blockchain interactions, enabling players to securely manage their assets.

## Tech Stack

The game is built with Unity and uses [Photon Fusion](https://www.photonengine.com/fusion) for multiplayer networking. On the blockchain side, we use [Solidity](https://www.soliditylang.org/) for smart contract development and [Sequence SDK](https://sequence.xyz/) for wallet integration and blockchain interactions. This combination allows us to create a seamless gaming experience while leveraging the benefits of blockchain technology for asset ownership and in-game economies.
